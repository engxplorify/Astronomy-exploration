木星

Jupiter

木星是太阳系最大的行星，主要由氢和氦构成，拥有强磁场和复杂的大气环流。其标志性的大红斑是一场长期存在的巨大风暴。通过地面望远镜可观测到木星云带变化，以及木卫一、木卫二等卫星的凌日与掩食现象。

Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System, composed mainly of hydrogen and helium, with a strong magnetic field and dynamic atmospheric circulation. Its Great Red Spot is a long-lived giant storm. Ground-based telescopes can observe cloud bands and moon transits such as Io and Europa.
